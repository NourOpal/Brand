08 — Rules

Do's & Don'ts

Non-negotiable. Reach out before publishing if unsure.

Do

✓ Use white logo on dark/black backgrounds
✓ Use black logo on white/light backgrounds
✓ Maintain clear space around the logo
✓ Match optical height in co-branded placements
✓ Display gems on black backgrounds only
✓ Use provided files in original format
✓ Refer to the app as "Opal" — capital O
✓ Reach out if you need a variation

Don't

✗ Place white logo on light backgrounds
✗ Place black logo on dark or busy photos
✗ Stretch, rotate, skew, or distort
✗ Apply shadows, glows, or effects
✗ Recolor beyond pure white or black
✗ Crop the logo or cut off elements
✗ Recreate the logo from scratch
✗ Use gradients as backgrounds
✗ Apply filters to any asset
✗ Overlay text on gem sculptures
✗ Screenshot or re-export from the app
✗ Imply endorsement beyond agreed scope
