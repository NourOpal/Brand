07 — Usage Rules

Do's & Don'ts

These rules are non-negotiable. Contact us before publishing if unsure.

✓ Do

✓ Use white logo on dark/black backgrounds
✓ Use black logo on white/light backgrounds
✓ Maintain clear space around the logo on all sides
✓ Match Opal logo to the same optical height as your logo in co-branded placements
✓ Display gem sculptures on black backgrounds only
✓ Use provided files in their original format
✓ Refer to the app as "Opal" — capital O
✓ Contact us if you need a variation or are unsure

✗ Don't

✗ Place white logo on light or pale backgrounds
✗ Place black logo on dark or busy photographs
✗ Stretch, rotate, skew, or distort the logo
✗ Apply shadows, glows, gradients, or effects to the logo
✗ Recolor the logo to anything other than pure white or black
✗ Crop the logo so any element is cut off
✗ Recreate the logo or wordmark from scratch
✗ Use gradient accent colors as backgrounds
✗ Apply filters or artistic treatments to any asset
✗ Overlay text on top of gem sculptures
✗ Screenshot or re-export assets from the app
✗ Use Opal assets to imply endorsement beyond agreed scope
